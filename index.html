Neil Patil


Hey there, I'm Neil. I just finished up studying Computer Science at The University of Texas at Austin. I'm interested in computer security and robotics.

I'm excited about startups. In a few months, I'll be joining Vanta in San Francisco, working to secure the internet and improve consumer trust. I spent last summer at Ike Robotics working on self-driving trucks. Before that, I spent summers at Facebook, Google, Indeed, and Atlassian.

In school, I was president of ISSS. We run national Capture-the-Flag competitions and teach basic security fundamentals. Our goal is to make security beginner-friendly, improve the culture and diversity of the cybersecurity community, and establish UT as a competitive security powerhouse.
